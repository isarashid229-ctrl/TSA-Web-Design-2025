Why this matters

Access saves lives.

When people can quickly find clinics, food, and hotlines—especially in Spanish—families get help faster and safer.

Offline counts during storms.

Power outages and weak cell service are common. The SOS kit gives neighbors printed numbers and checklists that work without internet.

Jobs & training change long-term outcomes.

Career centers, GED, ESL, and apprenticeships build stability. We surface free and low-cost options first.
