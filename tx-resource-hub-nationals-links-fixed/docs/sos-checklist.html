Emergency checklist

Keep this page saved or printed. Works offline.

Go-bag (72 hours)

  • Water (1 gal/person/day)
  • Non-perishable food + can opener
  • Flashlight + extra batteries
  • First-aid kit + prescriptions
  • Power bank + cords
  • Cash, keys, ID
  • Warm clothes + blanket

Documents (copies)

  • IDs, passports, birth certificates
  • Insurance (home/auto/health)
  • Medication list & providers
  • Bank / account info
  • Emergency contacts list

Home prep

  • Turn off gas, water, electricity (if advised)
  • Secure heavy furniture
  • Fire extinguisher & smoke alarms
  • Pet supplies / carriers

Contacts & info

  • Family meet-up place
  • Out-of-state contact
  • Local shelter / evacuation info
  • Utility emergency numbers
